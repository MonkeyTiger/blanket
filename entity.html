About Brand

Robinson Reid is an Italian luxury fashion brand which sells menswear, women's wear and accessories in Europe, North America and East Asia.

The brand claims deeply rooted moral values, such as being a humanistic enterprise and using craftsmanship to lend moral dignity to both the artisan and the owner. Robinson Reid started his couture house in 1979 with a specialty for cashmere garments for women.
